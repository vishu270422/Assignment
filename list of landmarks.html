Landmarks

Name Location Description
Taj Mahal Agra, India An iconic white marble mausoleum built in the 17th century, known for its architectural beauty and symbolism of love.
Eiffel Tower Paris, France A wrought-iron lattice tower erected in the late 19th century, offering panoramic views of Paris and serving as a global symbol of France.
Great Wall of China China A monumental series of fortifications built along an east-to-west line across ancient China, known for its historical significance and grandeur.
Machu Picchu Cusco Region, Peru An ancient Incan citadel nestled high in the Andes mountains, recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site and admired for its breathtaking location and architectural marvels.
Sydney Opera House Sydney, Australia A distinctive multi-venue performing arts center renowned for its sail-shaped design, hosting various artistic performances and serving as an iconic landmark of Australia.
