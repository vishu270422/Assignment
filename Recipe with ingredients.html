Kadai Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 2 cloves
  • 1tsp fennel Seeds
  • 1 tsp Jeera
  • 4 pepper corns
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Seeds
  • 2 Cardamoms
  • 2 onions ( cut lengthwise)
  • 1 big Tomato ( chopped )
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 capsicum ( diced)
  • 1 baby onions (cubed)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 green Chillies ( cut lengthwise )
  • 1 tsp Aamchur Powder
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 250 gms Paneer

Instructions:

  1. Roast coriander seeds, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, cloves, cardamoms, pepper corns in a heated pan for about a minute.
  2. Open the cardamom pods and let the seeds remain in the mixture
  3. Let the mixture cool
  4. Heat some oil in a pan, add the onions to it and saute
  5. Meanwhile grind the dry roasted seeds in a grinder to make a smooth powder
  6. Once the onions turns brown, add the tomatoes and cook it till the tomatoes turn soft
  7. Add salt and mix everything well
  8. Put the content in the grinder with the powdered spices in it.
  9. Add raw mango powder and grind it to make a smooth paste.
  10. Add water if needed.
  11. Put butter in a pan and add ginger and garlic paste to it
  12. Mix well ginger and garlic before the butter melts
  13. Add chillies and saute it with the paste
  14. Add shelled onions and toss them and cook them for about 2 mins
  15. Add diced capsicum to it and add the masala paste once the onions have turned translucent
  16. Cook everything for a min and add water to it.
  17. Stir it in and let it boil
  18. Add the paneer cubes, toss them up and mix well everything.
  19. Add salt and coriander leaves to it and mix well
  20. The gravy dish is ready to be served hot with roti / paratha

Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, washed and torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions:

  1. Place the torn lettuce in a large salad bowl.
  2. Sprinkle the croutons over the lettuce.
  3. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the salad, starting with a small amount and adding more as desired.
  4. Toss the salad gently to coat the lettuce and croutons with the dressing.
  5. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  6. Serve immediately as a side dish or add grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a piece of parchment paper.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese and tomato slices on top of the sauce.
  6. Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the pizza.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Carefully transfer the parchment paper with the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  9. Bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone mat.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
