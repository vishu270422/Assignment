Famous Historical Battles

  • Battle of Plassey

    Date:23 June,1757

    Description:The Battle of Plassey was a significant battle in the history of India,where the British East India Company defeated Siraj ud-Daulah,the last independent Nawab of Bengal.This battle marked the beginning of British dominance.

  • Battle of Panipat

    Date:14 January,1761

    Description:The Battle of Panipat was fought between the Marathas and the Durrani Empire led by Ahmad Shah Durrani.The Marathas suffered a decisive defeat in this battle,which had a significant impact on the political landscape of India.

  • Battle of Plassey

    Date:2 October,1864

    Description:The Battle of Buxar was fought between the British East India Company and the combined forces of Mir Qasim,Shuja-ud-Daulah,and Shah Alam II.The British emerged victorious in this battle,further consolidating their rule.

  • Battle of Seringapatam

    Date:4 May,1799

    Description:The Battle of Seringapatam was a major conflict fought between the British East India Company and the Kingdom of Mysore under Tipu Sultan.The British forces,led by Arthur Wellesley,succeeded in capturing the capital city of Seringapatam and defeating Tipu Sultan.

  • Battle of Plassey

    Date:22 June,1758

    Description:The Battle of Wandiwash was fought between the British East India Company and the French East India Company.The British emerged victorious,securing their control over the Carnatic region and weakening French influence in India.
